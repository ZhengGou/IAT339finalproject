Small shooting game: Undersea

Introduction

Spring 2016 - This is the final project of Introduction Game Design course, I am presenting my shooting game project named "Undersea". The function of this game is to control the fish character to shoot the enemies who block his way to find a special flower for his ill mother. Throughout the levels, the enemies will get larger and stronger throughout the levels, they will have higher lifespan, harder to kill, and their weapons will become more powerful. The goal of this game is that the playerâ€™s character should overcome the challenge, destroy all the enemies and pick-up the flower at the end of the game.

My role: visual designer, interaction designer,programmer.

Process

Create setting

The game have three levels. Player who is the fish will go to next level by kill all the enemies in current level. In order to get through all levels, player has to kill all the enemies in each level. There are three types of enemies, which are Enemy, SmallBossEnemy, and BigbossEnemy.

UML Diagram

Making and gradually completing an uml diagram is very necessary for game design, because the uml diagram can help us think about the logical relationship between object and its method. In this uml diagram, the arrow represents possession, the prism represents inheritance, and the "+" icon means public methods.

Breakdown levels

The first level is a particularly simple shot of the enemy. In game psychology, designers often let players get in touch with very simple levels at the outset, giving them a sense of accomplishment and interest in the game. In this level, my fish will only spit slow speed bubbles to attack players, players can easily win at the first level.
