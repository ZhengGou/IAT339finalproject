Interaction web Design: The maid

Introduction

Spring 2019 - As a team of four in this Interaction web Design course, we are presenting our final term project named "The maid". The maid is a interaction web base text explore game which the targeted audiences are both males and females aged between 15 to 45, who are interesting with spend some time on text-image game. The main idea of the website is to allow user to have immersion experience by interaction with the image.

My role: visual designer, interaction designer. I create the flow chart of interaction.

Create setting

According to our research, we found out that the average number of best friends and good friends of every Canadian are getting lower throughout the years. Most people preferred to keep in touch with each other through social media apps without actually hanging out together. In fact, the lack of ‘face-to-face’ interaction will result in less close friends.

We also found that since people start making friends on social media, the struggle of ﬁnding a real friend has become more obvious. People are struggling to be very real since they don’t really know each other very much or even never met before in real life. Thus, they will be a limit to the number of people which they can keep a quality friendship with, such as the type of friend they can phone for advice or help.
