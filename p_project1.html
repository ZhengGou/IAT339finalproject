01

The Maid

As a team of four in this Interaction web Design course, we are presenting our final term project named "The maid". The maid is an interactive web base on text explore the game in which the targeted audiences are both males and females aged between 15 to 45, who are interesting in spending some time on the text-image games. The main idea of ​​the website is to allow user to have immersion experience by interaction with the image.

02

Process

my teammates and I created a story together. After the discussion, we decided to create a moodboard based on our existing story to let others know the atmosphere of our story. At the same time, we also created room orientation maps to guide us to think more about the interactive elements of the corresponding room. In order to organize interactive elements and storylines, we also draw a flowchart to sort out ideas. After clarifying the storyline, we created a visual storyboard flowchart to allow us to create this web game more quickly.

Moodboard

In the beginning, the professor asked us to think about the character set. We found that we were very interested in this role-playing case. Through discussion, we decided to make this text-based web page. First of all, we have created a moodboard to make it easier for us to combine the script with this idea.

Room orientation maps

My teammates drew room orientation maps, and I participated in the discussion of room orientation.

Flowchart

I made a flow chart based on the script, and I used the way of illustration to illustrate the logic of our web page exploring interaction. Because we decided to make this web game on wix, I found the corresponding icon to display the items that can be clicked. I think the visual expression helps my team members to make and improve the story line more stereoscopically. We also created a flowchart of the storyboard version to get a more intuitive understanding of how the story interacts.

Visual storyboard flowchart

My team member drew this visual storyboard flowchart. After discussing with the instructor, we felt that some storylines are unnecessary, and because our background is too huge, we decided to reduce unnecessary stories while maintaining playability. Branches, for example, we deleted the storyline of the owner son and we simplified some clickable interactive branches.

Visual pictures

I created most images of interactive elements. In this part, I think it is more difficult to find the material. Pictures in high definition that fit our story are often difficult to find and usually take a few days. Editing these pictures took one of my team members and I about two weeks.

Interactive element

In addition to making visual pictures, I also participated in the creation of some interactive elements. This part is very challenging because you need to hide the click event behind the picture. My team members and I didn't know how to do it in wix in the beginning. After discussing and searching for information on the Internet, we discovered this approach. In our game, users can click on the corresponding items to explore the information. We believe that this will bring an immersive interactive experience to users.

03

Outcome

I think this project was successful because the final game did meet our expectations. I also learned how to communicate with team members when opinions are not consistent in this project. This communication ability and teamwork ability have greatly helped my future teamwork. At the same time, this project has also trained my graphic design skills, and also made me more familiar with using Adobe software.
